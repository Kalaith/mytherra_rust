About This Game

You are a minor deity watching one living world — a world many other deities watch too. You don't command mortals. You nudge regions toward prosperity or ruin, cultivate champions from the hero roster, forge artifacts, shape weather and myth, sway the pantheon, and wager at the Divine Observatory on what the world will do next.

Everything unfolds on its own: regions fracture, conquer, and found new frontiers; heroes migrate, earn renown, and pass into legend; gods stir as their domains rise; and every hundred-year age ends in a cataclysm, collapse, conquest, magical rupture, or divine war that remakes the map. Your favor is limited — spend it where it counts, and read the portents before the age turns.

Controls

  • MouseSwitch tabs, select regions/heroes, take divine actions
  • SpaceAdvance the world one year
  • SSave the world
  • LLoad the saved world
  • NBegin a new world

The Settings screen also offers auto-tick pacing, so the years can turn on their own while you watch and intervene.
